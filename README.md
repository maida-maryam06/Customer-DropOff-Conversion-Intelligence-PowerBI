A multi-page **Power BI analytics dashboard** designed to analyze customer journey behavior in an e-commerce environment.  
The project focuses on identifying **conversion bottlenecks, cart abandonment patterns, revenue leakage, and high-risk customer segments** using simulated real-world data.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze full customer purchase funnel (Visit ‚Üí Cart ‚Üí Checkout ‚Üí Purchase)
- Measure overall conversion performance
- Detect cart abandonment behavior
- Estimate revenue loss due to customer drop-off
- Identify high-risk customer segments
- Analyze customer value and geographic conversion trends

---

## Dataset Overview

The dataset was **synthetically generated using Python** to simulate realistic e-commerce customer behavior.

**Total Records:** 3,500+

### Key Fields

- `User_ID`
- `Traffic_Source` (Google, Facebook, Email, Direct, Referral)
- `Device` (Desktop, Mobile, Tablet)
- `Country`
- `User_Age` & `Age_Group`
- `Gender`
- `Product_Category`
- `Added_To_Cart`
- `Reached_Checkout`
- `Purchased`
- `Order_Value`
- `Abandoned_Cart`

## Key Insights

- Desktop users exhibit the **highest cart abandonment rate**.
- Google traffic produces **higher-quality conversions** compared to other sources.
- The **35‚Äì44 age group shows the strongest conversion performance**.
- A significant amount of revenue is lost due to **cart abandonment**.
- Mobile contributes heavily to **overall revenue leakage**.
